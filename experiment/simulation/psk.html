Instructions for Binary Phase Shift Keying Modulation (both Baseband and Passband)

  • Step 1: Click on 'Generate Message' button to generate input message signal
  • Step 2: Then click on 'Generate Carrier' button to generate carrier signal
  • Step 3: You can change the carrier signal frequency from the input fields
  • Step 4: Click on 'Simulate PSK' button to generate Phase Shift Keying Signal



Instructions for Biary Phase Shift Keying (BPSK) Demodulation

  • Step 1: You can Demodulate the FSK Signal clicking on the 'Demodulate PSK' button
  • Step 2: Click on the 'Generate BER vs SNR' button to plot BER vs SNR graph
  • Step 3: Click on the 'Generate Constellation' button to see the constellation diagram for the same
